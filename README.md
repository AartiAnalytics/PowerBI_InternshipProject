# PowerBI_InternshipProject
AI Job Market Analysis dashboard using Power BI showing salary trends, job demand, skills, and industry insights.

🤖 AI Job Market Analysis Dashboard

📖 The Story Behind This Project

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries across the world. New job roles are emerging rapidly, salaries are increasing, and companies are constantly searching for professionals with the right skills.

But this raises an important question:

What does the AI job market actually look like?

Which AI roles are most in demand?

What skills are companies looking for?

Which industries pay the highest salaries?

Are remote jobs becoming more common?

To answer these questions, I built an interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes AI job market data and converts raw information into meaningful insights.

This project explores the global AI job landscape, helping students, professionals, and organizations understand the trends shaping the future of AI careers.

🎯 Project Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze the AI job market and uncover insights related to:
Job demand across different roles
Salary trends across industries
Experience level requirements
Global job distribution
Work modes (Remote, Hybrid, Onsite)
Key technical skills required in AI careers.
Key Analytical Questions Addressed by the Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard was designed to explore the AI job market and answer several important analytical questions. Through interactive visualizations and filters, the dashboard helps analyze trends related to job demand, salaries, experience requirements, industries, and skills.

The dashboard enables users to answer the following key questions:
What is the total number of AI jobs available in the dataset?

What is the average salary offered in the AI job market?

What is the average experience required for AI roles?

Which company size (Small, Medium, Large) offers the most AI job opportunities?

Which industries provide the highest and lowest average salaries?

Which AI job roles are most in demand across industries?

How does salary vary across different experience levels?

Which countries have the highest number of AI job opportunities?

What is the distribution of employment types such as Full-time, Contract, Freelance, and Part-time?

What is the distribution of work modes including Remote, Hybrid, and Onsite jobs?

Which technical skills are most demanded in AI job postings?
📊 Dashboard Overview

The dashboard is divided into three main sections:

1️⃣ AI Job Market Overview

Provides a high-level summary of the job market.
Key insights include:
Total number of AI jobs
Average salary offered
Average experience required
Job distribution by company size
Salary comparison across industries
Most demanded AI roles

2️⃣ Salary & Experience Insights

This section focuses on how experience affects salary and job demand.
It includes:
Average salary by experience level
Top AI job roles
Job distribution by country
Industry filters
Experience level filters

This allows users to explore how career growth impacts earnings in the AI field.

3️⃣ Job Market Trends & Skill Demand
This section highlights industry trends and skill requirements.
Key insights include:
Remote vs Hybrid vs Onsite job distribution
Employment type distribution
Top skills required in AI jobs
Market salary trends across industries
Skills like Python, SQL, TensorFlow, Kubernetes, and Data Visualization appear as some of the most demanded skills.

🔎 Key Insights
Some interesting insights from the analysis:
The dataset contains ~45K AI job listings.
The average salary is around $115K, showing strong earning potential in AI careers.
Machine Learning Engineers, Data Scientists, and AI Architects are among the most demanded roles.
Python is the most frequently required skill.
AI jobs are distributed globally with strong demand across multiple countries.
Remote and hybrid work models are becoming increasingly common.

📌 Conclusion

The AI Job Market Analysis Dashboard transforms raw job data into actionable insights.
It helps understand how the AI job market is evolving, what skills are most valuable, and where opportunities are growing.
As AI continues to grow, data-driven insights like these will help professionals prepare for the careers of the future.
